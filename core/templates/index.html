Clustering Game Genres: Overview of Approach Objective: Reduce 23 game genres into 4 main clusters for analysis and categorization. Preprocessing Steps: Stopwords List: Fine-tuned to exclude irrelevant words and improve the clarity of clusters. Key tool for optimizing the final outcomes. Lemmatization: Standardized words to their base forms for consistent representation. Bag of Words: Converted text data into numerical vectors to feed into the model. Clustering Methodology: Used Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA) via Scikit-learn to identify clusters. Visualization Techniques: Wordclouds: Helped in understanding the dominant themes of each cluster. t-SNE: Provided a low-dimensional representation of clusters. pyLDAvis: Offered interactive exploration of cluster-topic relationships. Key Insights and Fine-tuning: Stopword list optimization was critical for achieving meaningful clusters. Visualizations played a significant role in interpreting and refining the clusters.
